ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project aims to analyze the relationship between latitude and key weather parameters, including temperature, cloudiness, and wind speed. The focus is on understanding temperature patterns approaching the equator, noting the southern hemisphere's warmer trend during this season. Additionally, the study explores intriguing cloudiness patterns and identifies notable wind speed occurrences in the northern hemisphere. The goal is to gain insights into the complex interplay between geographical location and weather variables, contributing to a global understanding of atmospheric dynamics.
