ABOUT THE PROJECT

The project is about flight on-time performance of the United States. The circles on the map represent the size of airport as well as location. On-Time perfromance is measured by the length of arrival/departure delay. The more delays an airport has, the worse performance it indicates. Delay reasons are broken down into 5 reasons, whcih are 'Aircraft', 'Carrier', 'Weather', 'NAS', 'Security'. Aircraft dealy is the cancellation or delay was due to circumstances within the airline's control. Carrier delay is a previous flight with same aircraft arrived late, causing the present flight to depart late.Weather dealy is significant meteorological conditions (actual or forecasted) that, in the judgment of the carrier, delays or prevents the operation of a flight. NAS stands for National Aviation System. It refers to delays and cancellations attributable to the national aviation system that refer to a broad set of conditions, such as non-extreme weather conditions, airport operations, heavy traffic volume, and air traffic control. Security delay is Delays or cancellations caused by evacuation of a terminal or concourse, re-boarding of aircraft because of security breach

The bar graph at the bottom shows the delay by airlines

The data source is from Bereau of Transportation Statistics : https://www.bts.gov/topics/airlines-and-airports-0
